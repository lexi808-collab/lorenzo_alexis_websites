About Us

Found in 1971 by brothers Stephen and Jimmy Tsukayama, Hawaiian Island Creations started as a small surfshop in Kailua on Oahu's Windward shore. Through a commitment to quality, service and aloha spirit, the brand has built a reputation as an icon of Hawaiian surfing recognized throughout the world. The retail chain has grown to include seventeen stores across three islands, providing authentic Hawaiian surf gear to both locals and visitors alike. Enjoy the ride wherever you maybe with HIC clothing and accessories shipped direct to you,-from Hawaii with aloha.Enjoy the ride!
